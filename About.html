About Serene Beans

Discover the journey behind your favorite cup of coffee

From Dream to Reality

Serene Beans was born from a simple passion: to create a space where coffee lovers could enjoy exceptional brews in a welcoming atmosphere. Founded in 2015 by three friends who shared a love for specialty coffee, our journey began in a small corner shop in downtown Gaborone.

What started as a modest operation with just one espresso machine and a handful of seats has grown into Botswana's premier coffee destination. Throughout our journey, our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community has never wavered.

Today, we continue to source the finest beans from ethical farms worldwide, roast them to perfection in our own facility, and serve them with pride and passion.
