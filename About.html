About Serene Beans

Our Story

The journey of Serene Beans and how we became a part of the community.

From Passion to Profession

Serene Beans was born in 2015 from a simple love for exceptional coffee and a desire to create a community space where people could slow down and enjoy quality moments.

Founded by siblings Thabo and Naledi Moremi, who grew up in the rich coffee regions of Botswana, we started as a small kiosk with just three types of coffee and a handful of pastries.

Fast forward to today, we've grown into Gaborone's favorite coffee destination, with our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community remaining at the heart of everything we do.

Our beans are ethically sourced from small-scale farmers across Africa and South America, and we roast them in small batches right here in our flagship store to ensure maximum freshness and flavor.
