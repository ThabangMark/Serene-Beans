Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Serene Beans.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering services for corporate events, parties, weddings, and other special occasions. We provide a variety of coffee options, pastries, and light meals. Please contact our catering team at catering@serenebeans.com for more information or fill out the contact form above.

Do you have Wi-Fi available?

Yes, we offer complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi for all our customers. Simply ask our staff for the password when you place your order.

Can I book a space for a small meeting?

Yes, we have a dedicated meeting space that can accommodate up to 10 people. It's equipped with a TV for presentations and reliable Wi-Fi. There's a minimum spend requirement of BWP 300. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to book the space.

Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes, we have a variety of vegan milk alternatives including soy, almond, oat, and coconut milk. We also offer gluten-free pastry options that are clearly marked on our menu. If you have specific dietary requirements, please let our staff know when placing your order.
